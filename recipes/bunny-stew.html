Bunny Stew

Description

Bunny Stew is a Crock Pot recipe exclusive to Don't Starve Together introduced in the An Eye for An Eye update. It requires 2 Ice and meat with total value less than 1. Using 1 full meat value or higher will result in Meatballs. Using less than two ice will also yield Meatballs.

Example Ingredients

  • Morsel x 1
  • Ice x 3

Steps

  1. Add Morsel to the Crock Pot.
  2. Add Ice to the Crock Pot.
  3. Wait for 10 seconds.
