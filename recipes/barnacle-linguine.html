Barnacle Linguine

Description

Barnacle Linguine is a Crock Pot recipe exclusive to Don't Starve Together introduced in Return of Them. It is made of 2 Barnacles or Cooked Barnacles and at least 2.0 Vegetable.

Example Ingredients

  • Barnacle x 2
  • Carrot x 2

Steps

  1. Add Barnacles to the Crock Pot.
  2. Add Carrots to the Crock Pot.
  3. Wait for 40 seconds.
